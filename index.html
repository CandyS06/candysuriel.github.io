About Me

Hi, I’m Candy Suriel, an islander from the Dominican Republic now navigating life in the United States.

My journey into the world of travel began during a university exchange program in Brazil, where I embraced the challenge of living independently in a vast country and immersing myself in a language that was new to me (although it was super similar).

This experience ignited my passion for exploration and cultural exchange, teaching me resilience and adaptability.

Professionally

Industrial Engineer with a MSc in Manufacturing and Mechanical and a minor in Quality.

I’ve built a career in Quality and Regulatory within the medical device industry.

I have successfully implemented Quality Management Systems (QMS) that ensure compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements.

My role has involved leading problem-solving initiatives aimed at identifying and addressing quality issues, ultimately enhancing product safety and efficacy.

I have also developed and managed processes to ensure adherence to applicable standards and regulations, including ISO 13485 and FDA regulations. This includes conducting audits, facilitating training, and promoting a culture of continuous improvement across teams.
